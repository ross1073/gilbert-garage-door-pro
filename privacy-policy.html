Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide to us directly, and information collected automatically when you visit our Site.

Information you provide directly: When you submit a service request or contact form on our Site, we collect the following:

  • Name — so we can address you properly and identify your request
  • Phone number — so a technician or scheduling coordinator can contact you to confirm your appointment

Information collected automatically: When you visit our Site, certain technical information is collected automatically by our web analytics tools, including:

  • Your IP address and approximate geographic location

  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your service requests. When you submit a contact or estimate form, your name, phone number, and message are used to contact you and arrange a service appointment or provide a quote.

  • To communicate with you. We may follow up via phone or email to confirm your appointment, provide service updates, or ask for feedback after your service is complete.
  • To improve our Site. Automatically collected data helps us understand how visitors use our Site so we can make it more useful and easier to navigate.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience and analyze how the Site is used. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by a website you visit. You can control cookies through your browser settings and certain opt-out mechanisms described below.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Google Analytics uses cookies to track visits anonymously. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can choose to set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, some features of our Site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
